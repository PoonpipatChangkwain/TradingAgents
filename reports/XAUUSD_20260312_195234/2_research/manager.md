Investors have viable alternatives that offer returns, making Gold's lack of yield a significant disadvantage.

While I acknowledge Gold's historical role as an inflation hedge and safe haven, the immediate market conditions, driven by robust technical sell signals and strong macroeconomic pressures, are simply too potent to ignore.
